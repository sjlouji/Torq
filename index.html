ABOUT THE DEPARTMENT

Mechanical Engineering Department was started with 60 students in the year 2010 and within a span of 4 years the strength has gone up to 550. The department has highly qualified, experienced and dedicated teaching and non-teaching faculty, some of them with more than 30 years of experience. There are 6 doctorates and well trained teachers to guide the students in project modeling and project management. Labs with state-of-art-facilities are an added advantage of the student community. LICET is one among the few colleges which has Automation lab to train the students in most advanced machining techniques.

Vison

To develop competent and socially responsible mechanical engineers by providing quality education and research facilities.

Mission

Department of Mechanical Engineering is committed
1. To collaborate with world class technical institutions both national, international and R&D organizations to impart practical training, teaching, research and entrepreneurship in order to prepare globally competitive mechanical engineers.
2. To provide state of the art infrastructure and research facilities and adopting modern teaching methodologies and training to achieve academic excellence.
3. To form socially responsible mechanical engineers by inculcating ethics through value based education.

About Torq

Department of Mechanical Engineering, LICET is organising its nineth National Level Technical Symposium (TORQ 2019) common for all the departments on 17th August, 2019. The Technical Symposium gives knowledge on the current trends in Engineering. The objective of the symposium is to help the students to exhibit their technical skills and to enlighten their technical knowledge. This symposium will pave the way for our budding engineers to transform their dreams in to reality and to build a strong INDIA.
